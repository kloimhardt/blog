> Michaelina Wautier in her Self-portrait, she depicts herself with a palette reduced to just a view colours. In ancient times, mastering such a limited colour palette was considered a sign of artistic superiority.
>
> Description of the 1649 painting at the Wautier exhibition in the Vienna Art Museum
